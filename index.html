CFO Agreement Details

Scope of Work

  • Record and organize expenses, sales, and pricing.
  • Calculate profits and losses based on the data you provide.

Data Sharing

Agree on how you’ll provide the necessary details (e.g., via spreadsheets, notes, or software).

Reporting

Set a schedule for when you’ll share profit/loss summaries (weekly, monthly, etc.).

Confidentiality

Ensure you agree not to share or misuse any business data provided.

Boundaries

Make it clear that you have no authority to make purchases, access accounts, or change pricing without approval.

Exit Agreement

Decide what happens if you can’t continue or if I no longer want you as CFO.
